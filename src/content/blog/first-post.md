This is the first post on the site.

The goal is simple: keep a personal place on the web that is easy to maintain,
easy to publish, and useful enough to return to. Posts live as Markdown files,
so writing a new one should feel closer to taking notes than operating a system.

Over time, this can become a record of projects, technical ideas, book notes,
and thoughts that benefit from a little more structure.
